enterprise paths:
- Candidate Selection
  - Compare 5-10 final candidates for a senior role
  - Categories: Technical skills, culture fit,
  compensation expectations, start date availability,
  referral strength, interview performance
  - Multi-interviewer weighting (hiring manager 10,
  peer 7, HR 5)
  - Export decision to ATS/offer letter
- Benefits Provider Selection
  - Health insurance, 401k, dental/vision vendors
  - Annual RFP process (predictable, high-stakes)
  - Categories: Cost per employee, coverage quality,
  network size, admin burden, employee satisfaction
- Software Vendor Selection (HUGE market)
  - Compare CRM options (Salesforce vs HubSpot vs
  Pipedrive)
  - ERP systems, HR platforms, dev tools
  - Categories: Pricing (TCO over 3 years), features,
  integration complexity, vendor stability, migration
  effort, customer support
  - Compliance: SOC 2, GDPR requirements auto-flagged
- Office Lease Comparison
  - 3-5 building options when relocating
  - Categories: Cost per sq ft, commute time,
  amenities, lease terms, expansion potential, parking
  - Real estate teams do this quarterly
- Bank/Lender Selection
  - Business loans, credit lines, treasury management
  - Categories: Interest rate, fees, relationship
  manager quality, approval speed, covenants
- Build vs Buy Decisions
  - "Should we build auth in-house or use Auth0?"
  - Categories: Upfront cost, ongoing maintenance, time
   to market, team expertise, strategic control,
  security risk
  - Engineering leaders make these monthly
- Tech Stack Selection
  - Database choice (Postgres vs MongoDB vs DynamoDB)
  - Cloud provider (AWS vs GCP vs Azure)
  - Categories: Performance, cost, team familiarity,
  ecosystem maturity, vendor lock-in risk
- Feature Prioritization
  - Compare 10+ roadmap items
  - Categories: Revenue impact, engineering effort,
  customer requests, strategic alignment, competitive
  necessity
  - Replaces RICE/ICE scoring spreadsheets
- Agency Selection
  - PR agency, creative agency, SEO firm comparison
  - Categories: Portfolio quality, industry experience,
   pricing, team seniority, case study results,
  chemistry
  - Marketing teams do this 2-3x per year
- Event Sponsorship Decisions
  - Which conferences to sponsor ($50K+ decisions)
  - Categories: Attendee count, target audience fit,
  speaking opportunities, competitor presence, booth
  cost, past lead quality
- Sales Territory Expansion
  - "Which region to enter next: EU, APAC, or LATAM?"
  - Categories: Market size, regulatory complexity,
  language barriers, competitive landscape, hiring
  difficulty
